2015 NCAAF Vegas Bias

Would you make money betting $100 on FSU to cover every game this season? Sure, they may end up with 10+ wins, but do they cover enough to turn a profit?

What about $100 that Alabama will always win straight-up? They're normally a big favorite, so your $100 risk may only win $5. Are you better off looking for a team like Kansas State that is just quietly having a better than expected year.

What about those really high over/unders for the Pac-12 and Big 12? Do those conferences normally go over or under?

Below is the data for the 2015 season. Let me know if you see any issues with the data. If you look closely you'll see a lot of interesting patterns.
