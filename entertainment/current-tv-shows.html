Television I'm currently watching

A list of the television shows I'm currently watching. Americans can stream each one. Let me know what I'm missing.

Archer

Cleverly stupid. Animated comedy about a bunch of dysfunctional spies.

Awake

A detective jumps between two worlds: one with his wife dead and other with his son dead. Just watch this trailer and you'll know whether it's up your alley.

Malcolm in the Middle

Why didn't I watch this more during its run? On par with my all time favorite 30-minute comedies: Scrubs and Arrested Development.

Revenge

I don't know why I like this one, but I keep watching it. It's a mindless way to relax.

Psych

Season 3 secured it as the most consistently funny hour-long show I've seen in a long time. Now rounding Season 6 (2012) it hasn't lost a step.

Battlestar Gallatica

A cult hit that I never got into during its original run. Luckily Netflix has every episode. I have trouble getting into many episodes, but anything involving Gaius is captivating.

South Park

The show that never lets me down. By now you know whether you like it or not. Every episode freely and legally available at southparkstudios.com

Burn Notice

Another mindless way to unwind after work.

Eureka

A street-smart dad sheriffs a town of scientific brainiacs through disasters of their own creation.

The Walking Dead

A zombie series filmed in or around Atlanta. I'm so watching.

Mad Men

Ad-men know how to sell cool.
