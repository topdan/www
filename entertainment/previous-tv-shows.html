Television I used to watch

A list of notable television shows I have watched. Try some of them and let me know ones I should try.

Scrubs

Scrubs is one of my all time favorites. Great mix between smart, silly, heartfelt and absurd.

The Wire

The most captivating and believable characters and world ever put on television, maybe.

Arrested Development

No matter how many times I watch, I find a new joke.

Friday Night Lights

The second season almost lost me, but it came back strong for 3-5.

Gilmore Girls

No it wasn't just because they're pretty. Quick, sharp, and quirky.

Finally a show about rednecks that shows they're human and doesn't just make fun of their ignorance.

Jericho

Life in post-nuclear war. Only two seasons but they were given enough warning to conclude the series well.

Deadwood

The writing is almost Shakespearean but with a lot more cussing. It lost me on the third season and I never finished, but the first two are great.

Firefly

Every episode of its short run is unbelievably good. There was also a movie: Serenity

Veronica Mars

The start of Kristen Bell's rise to stardom was this cute, young detective comedy-drama.

The Tick

Not for everyone but Puddy from Seinfeld plays the want-to-be superhero.

Futurama

Was the heir-apparent to The Simpsons until the Fox executives decided to cancel everything they could in the early 2000's. I haven't caught up with the new run on Comedy Central yet.

Weeds

The first couple seasons were fun, then it got ridiculous. I'm convinced the head writers hate men.

True Blood

How did the writer of American Beauty also make this? Not bad for what it is: a supernatural sex-drama.
